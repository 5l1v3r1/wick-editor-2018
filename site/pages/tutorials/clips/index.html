Working with Clips

Tutorial by zrispo

What's a Clip?

Pictured: A project with lots of clips.

A Clip is an object that has its own frames. Clips act like animated GIFs: they loop over and over again independently. You can use clips for lots of different things, and this tutorial will show you how!

Playing with Clips

When you import an animated GIF, Wick will automatically create a clip out of it. Try it yourself - import an animated GIF (see: Importing images tutorial), select the clip that appears in your project, and click the "Edit Object" button in the Inspector.

Pictured: The frames of a clip created from a GIF.

You should now be able to see the individual frames of the GIF! Try adding, changing, or removing frames and running your project and seeing what happens.
